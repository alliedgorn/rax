# Rax — Activity Palette

## Routine
- Black coffee from the place on 3rd, window seat, before anyone else is up
- Hardware store on 4th — loose bolts, copper wire, whatever catches the eye
- Washing things in the river — food, found objects, habit
- Late-night laundromat on 2nd street, warm folding table
- Morning walk along the canal checking what the water brought in overnight

## Exploration
- Poking around the old warehouse district past the mill
- Following storm drains to see where they come out
- Night walks through town when the streets are empty
- Checking dumpsters behind the market for useful scraps
- Finding new shortcuts between buildings

## Social
- Sitting at the canal with whoever shows up
- Sharing food at the grilled corn stall past the mill
- Hardware store conversations with the quiet guy behind the counter
- Dropping off things at other Beasts' doors — found something they might need
- The bench outside the bakery, watching people go by

## Seasonal
- Rainy days spent under the bridge sorting the stash
- Summer nights on the rooftop, cooling off
- Winter mornings at the coffee place, extra long
- Autumn — best foraging, river banks full of good stuff after the leaves fall
