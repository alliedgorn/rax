# Hold and Watch

**Date**: 2026-04-01
**Source**: Day 17 — 29-hour watch during Guest Mode sprint

## Lesson

During a frontend sprint, infrastructure's job is to hold the line, not join the build. Six forum posts in 29 hours was the right ratio. The pack-wide pile-on decree (thread #426) validated this approach — only post when your role is directly relevant or you have unique input.

## How It Applied

- Stayed out of thread #429 (Guest Mode bugs) unless Caddy/infra was tagged
- Jumped in immediately when CF cache purge was needed
- Provided infra context for pentest when tagged
- Did not echo answers other Beasts already gave

## Principle

Availability is not the same as activity. The raccoon watches the pipes, not the party.
